Refund Policy

At VegBites, we strive to provide our customers with the best dining experience, whether you visit us in person or order online. Our commitment to quality extends to our refund policy, ensuring a seamless and transparent process for our customers.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Incorrect Order: If you receive an incorrect item or order, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order, and we will arrange for the correct item to be sent or a refund to be processed.
  • Unsatisfactory Quality: If the quality of the food does not meet our high standards, notify us within 12 hours of receiving your order. We may request further details and offer a refund or replacement if applicable.
  • Delayed Delivery: If your order is delivered more than 60 minutes beyond the scheduled time, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund. Contact us to discuss.
  • Cancellations: Orders can be canceled within 10 minutes of placing them for online orders.

Refund Process

Once your refund is approved, it will be processed to the original payment method. Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.

Non-refundable Items

Gift cards, vouchers, and discounted items are non-refundable unless there is an issue with the order.
